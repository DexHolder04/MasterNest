Welcome to The Master Nest! This is the ultimate resource for Pokémon Go nests. We offer a list of the top worldwide nests organized by Pokémon species, separated by generation; plus a GPX section where you can get a custom route for the nest you wish to play on. Hop around the pages and start hunting!

Our home and base of operations is the CoolDown Club discord server, which you can access [here]. You can find organized raids, rare Pokémon spawns, mini-games and much more! Plus it is the best place to report any discrepancies you may find in this report, as we manually research and submit each nest each migration.

And keep in mind the following list, these are the only species that nest, any other Pokémon is only obtainable through regular spawns, eggs, research or raids!

This is migration 179. This one was Pikachu with a hat.

Hey everyone! Hope you're doing well! Our report for this migration is now live, and thanks to the ongoing TCG Event there's some interesting changes with the nest pool.

Las week, the TCG Event triggered a frameshift migration that changed the nest pool, making part of our report invalid. My intention was to have our report updated as soon as that happened, but unfortunately I was feeling a bit under the weather at the time. However, this time was useful to cross-reference and establish the new changes.

And speaking of which, we have Pikachu (wearing the TCG hat), Eevee, Aipom and Alolan Rattata back in the nest pool, at least for the duration of the event. Some other species were removed from the pool, namely Spinarak, Bidoof and Pidove; but they're freaking everywhere thanks to the event so there's no shortage of them.

These changes may be reverted at the end of the TCG Event, but hopefully the pool will stay the same (just adding back the removed species). What is certain is that there will be a frameshift migration at the end of the event when the customed Pikachu is removed from the wild, and this time we'll definitely be getting an update up once that happens!

We're pretty confident in this one, but of course you can always report a discrepancy over our Discord server [here]. We are human, after all!

Hopefully you find our report useful! We'll see you soon.

Thanks for stopping by; stay healthy and happy hunting!

-Dex
