This is migration 177b. This one was a shift in seasons.

Hey everyone! Hope you're doing well! We're back with an update to our report, now that the Season of GO is live worldwide.

Our sample size is not the largest, so we are quite sure we haven't covered all the current nesting species (especially in the southern hemisphere), and so we recommend you keep an eye out in the coming weeks as we cross reference with other reports to determine which species are native to which hemisphere and gather a more complete report.

As I said, we'll continue to add more findings to our report over the coming days, ideally before the Adventure Week Event starts on June 7, to try to get the most accurate read for the nesting pool before it potentially gets changed during the event.

But so far, it seems like we have some interesting newcomers in Grimer, Krabby, Zubat and Trubbish. These can all be shiny and would be nice to hunt in big nests, and in particular Trubbish is great for stardust farming so it's definitely worth a trip or two even if you don't manage to find the shiny (I know I'm still missing it TT).

Again, we'll keep updating the report in the coming days, but of course you can always report a discrepancy over our Discord server [here]. We are human, after all!

Hopefully you find our report useful, at least for now! We'll see you soon.

Thanks for stopping by; stay healthy and happy hunting!
