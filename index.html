A Nest Report


Welcome to The Master Nest! This is the ultimate resource for Pokémon Go nests. We offer a list of the top worldwide nests organized by Pokémon species, separated by generation; plus a GPX section where you can get a custom route for the nest you wish to play on. Hop around the pages and start hunting!

Our home and base of operations is the CoolDown Club discord server, which you can access [here]. You can find organized raids, rare Pokémon spawns, mini-games and much more! Plus it is the best place to report any discrepancies you may find in this report, as we manually research and submit each nest each migration.

And keep in mind the following list, these are the only species that nest, any other Pokémon is only obtainable through regular spawns, eggs, research or raids!

This is migration 186. This one was fashionably early.

Hey everyone! We're back with a quicker-than-usual update.

This time around, the regularly-scheduled migration ocurred right after the Fashion Week event went live worldwide. Thanks to this, we have a good idea of how the event is affecting the nesting pool, and as it turns out, both Croagunk and Diglett have been removed from nests for the duration of the event.

This is part of the reason why I rushed this report a little bit, because once the event wraps up all over the world both Croagunk and Diglett will return to nests causing our report to become partially invalid. So I wanted to have the site up and running during the event, and once the frameshift migration happens we'll update the report as soon as possible.

So that's our report for now! We think we did a good job, but as always you can report a discrepancy over our Discord server [here]. We are human, after all!

Hopefully you find our report useful! We'll see you soon.

Thanks for stopping by; stay healthy and happy hunting!
