Welcome to The Master Nest! This is the ultimate resource for Pokémon Go nests. We offer a list of the top worldwide nests organized by Pokémon species, separated by generation; plus a GPX section where you can get a custom route for the nest you wish to play on. Hop around the pages and start hunting!

Our home and base of operations is the CoolDown Club discord server, which you can access [here]. You can find organized raids, rare Pokémon spawns, mini-games and much more! Plus it is the best place to report any discrepancies you may find in this report, as we manually research and submit each nest each migration.

And keep in mind the following list, these are the only species that nest, any other Pokémon is only obtainable through regular spawns, eggs, research or raids!

This is migration 173. This one was sent blasting off again.

Hey everyone! Long time no see! Last migration was a bit messy, I got really busy and couldn't really find the time to sit down and write up a blog like I usually do. And then as the days went by I just plain forgot, sorry about that! Not sure how many of you guys actually read these, but I enjoy writing them so thank you for taking the time to read them!

This time around, migration ocurred right before the All-hands Rocket Retreat Event. This impacted nests in no way that we could tell, but I still wanted to wait a bit just to be sure.

This event is all about Rocket stops and new shadow Pokémon kidnapped in them. It's a good opportunity to hunt for a good Swampert and Machamp, both are great either for PvP and PvE. Latias is the new shadow legendary, many would encourage you to skip this one and save your radar for a more "useful" shadow legend but honestly she cute so you betcha I'm gonna catch it. Oh and you can TM away Frustration, that's always useful.

But in regards to nests, everything's pretty much normal! So we really hope you find our report useful, as always please feel free to report any inaccuracies or new findings over our Discord server [here].

Thanks for stopping by; stay healthy and happy hunting!
